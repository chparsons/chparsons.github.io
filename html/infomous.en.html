Infomous Inc. is a start up based in Cambridge, Massachussetts and NY. It develops a web app that lets you navigate content through an interactive network visualization. Users grab data from the web and create clouds/networks to navigate the information:

"Infomous is an exciting new way to navigate and discover online content. Infomous summarizes the content of one or more feeds and renders the results as an interactive word cloud, which displays the relative relationships of the words to each other.

Infomous is a creation of Icosystem Corp. a company dedicated to predictive analytics using agent-based modeling.

"Icosystem’s predictive models replicate the detailed behavior of real systems–marketplaces, supply chains, business organizations, or social networks–whose complexity goes beyond the reach of traditional analytical approaches."
