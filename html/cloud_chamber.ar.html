This installation is made by modules that can be replicated and freely grouped in space to fit any space dimensions.

Each module is made of:

  • Spiral structures made with translucent fabric.

  • Projection of real-time graphics over the structures: a particle system flowing in space is projected. It resembles wind, dust, water, stars, ...

    The shape of the structures and its translucency makes the particles float in 3D space achieving an holographic effect.

  • Physical interaction device: a low stage located at about 4 mt of distance from the spirals defines the interaction area.

    It contains a projector that points to the spirals and it's illuminated from above with a rounded sharp light beam so people can clearly put their hands into and out of the space delimited by the light.

    When people wave their hands inside the interaction area they swirl the projected particles as if they were touching them.
