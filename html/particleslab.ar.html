Concept.

Particles-Lab is a low-cost mobile lab-in-a-box for people to tinker with complex adaptive systems through tangible-computation. An interactive construction kit that projects an agent-based particle system simulation on top of physical building blocks using 3d camera-projector technology. A playground for people to build 3D structures in space with low-cost materials using their own hands and transform them into computational artifacts on-the-fly. This learning tool enables physical and ludic exploration of complex systems concepts like self-organization, emergence, decentralized decision making, collective behaviors, non-linearity, networks, evolution and adaptation. A tool for the development of human creativity, expression, computational thinking and learning that bridges the digital and physical worlds.

Vision.

There are low-cost laptops for children to learn computation, but they are based on the Personal Computer + GUI model. There's a need for a low-cost mobile device for children to learn Tangible-Computation.
