The project explores data visualization and:

» collaboration

"...the technology came to us: from computers on our desktops to laptops to mobile phones.

While these technologies lowered the barrier to communicating and accessing information, they created other barriers. We are now often greeted in meetings with a literal barrier of screens that get in the way of face-to-face communication.

Can we instead make devices that encourage in-person, face-to-face communication — while still delivering the data people need, only when they need it?"


Thad Starner on wired
