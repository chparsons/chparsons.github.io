Visualization Experiments.

Money and political networks.

d3 javascript

This is a prototype for a visualization of the political and financial networks in Argentina. It's based on accountings submitted by political parties and electoral alliances. Here you can see 3 types of agents related:

  • Private Contributors

    • People

    • Companies

  • Money Receivers

    • Political Groups


Each graph displays the biggest 400 contributors.

Node size represents the total amount of money contributed by a person or company to one or more political organizations.

Click on a node for more info.



https://github.com/chparsons/vizlab/


How are our representative democracies working? How much democratic and representative are they if they depend on funding with highly concentrated origins? By whom and for whom are these democracies working? Are we living more on plutocracies rather than democracies?


Being a protoype there's a lot to fix and improve.

It's interesting to see how the composition of political funding is revealed, its structure and distribution of companies/people.

Besides qualitative analysis, at a quantitative level, the direct relation between amount of money available for parties and election results is also visible.

It's also interesenting to notice how certain nodes contribute to different groups acting as links between different clusters.
